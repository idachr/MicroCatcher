Privacy Policy

At Micro Cather we take your privacy very seriously, and work to minimize the personal information we process. On this page you can read what information we collect, how we protect your information and how you can contact us.

Micro Cather is established as a Norwegian startup. We are located at Slottsplassen 1, O100 Oslo, Norway. Our data processing is in compliance with the Norwegian Personal Data Act and the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Micro Catcher is the data controller of the information we collect and process. Micro Catcher is established as a Norwegian startup. We are located at Slottsplassen 1, O100 Oslo, Norway.

We aim is to always limit the amount of personal data we process and protect your and our information by using data security standards such as ISO 27000. We use Microsoft as a data processor/platform supplier for our internal systems. Our data is only processed in their data center in Norway. We do not use any market analysis and we do not track anything on our website.

When you contact us, we will this information as a record in our Customer Relationship Management system as a contact. When you buy a Micro Catcher you become our customer and we will keep records of our communication, agreement and payments. We do not sell or share your personal information with anyone. We publish data on many of the Micro Catchers on our web-site.

As a customer you decide how Micro Catcher share information about your Micro Catchers, its location, its micro plastic collection and your ownership. Many customers are happy to let us share the Micro Catchers data on our web site, but this is your decision.

If you wish to contact us with regard to how we ensure your privacy, please email Micro@Catcher.com

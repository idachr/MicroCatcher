Problem

In 2017 there was an estimate of 150 million tons of plastic waste in the ocean. Every year, about 8 million tons of plastic are thrown into the ocean. This is equivalent to a garbage truck filled with plastic dumped in the ocean every minute. If this rate continues we will have more plastic than fish in our oceans by 2050.

What is microplastic?

Microplastics are small pieces of plastic that fish and other sea creatures will confuse with food like plankton or other fish. When the microplastic gets eaten it will travel up the food chain and end up on our dinner plates.

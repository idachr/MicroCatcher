We are
Micro Catcher!

Micro Catcher is a new and innovative organization with the goal to clean the sea for future generations. We want to clean the sea of all plastic and other rubbish that affects marine life. We will use new, innovative, and recyclable solutions to achieve our goals. Slowly but surely, the ocean will be cleaner by reusing the same materials that once damaged the ocean.

Every day we try to find new and innovative features to improve our product. We look at the problems of the past, present, and future to work as hard as we can to find realistic solutions. We have partners all over the world and often contact similar organizations for feedback on how our products can be improved. The challenge to have a clean sea can not be achieved alone and that is why we need your help.

Our main focus is microplastic, but that does not stop us from cleaning the sea of other materials. Our organization was established in the autumn of 2020 by four students with a strong desire to make the world a better place. We have used all of our skills to invent a product that could make a difference. After a lot of hard work and commitment, we are finally able to present our new product: Micro Catcher.

The Micro Catcher started as a solution to protect fish farming and clean up plastic and other rubbish around these areas. When we realized what our product was capable of doing, we decided to further implement the product so it could be used for more than just protecting a small proportion of the fish in the sea. Why only protect the fish in fish farms, when we could help protect all of them? Now we had a new goal in mind; to get it at sea.
